Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Augste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806[1] by the French Neoclassical artist Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre.It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date : 1806
Dimensions : 100 x 70 cm
Base : St. Germain-en-Laye Paris
